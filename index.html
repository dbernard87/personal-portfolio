About

Hello! I'm David Bernard, a student in the UNH Full Stack Bootcamp course. I started writing HTML in the late 90's during the Half Life modding days, and my passion for web design has continued to this day. I have expanded my knowledge of HTML, as well as learning CSS and Javascript. I also have knowledge of Wordpress based websites and hosting services. I continue to learn these important skills and always look forward to learning something new!

My goal is to build clean, functional, and responsive websites that work on any of today's changing technology. I understand the importance of making my work available to users on any device.
